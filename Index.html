About Tony Knox

Professional Photographer - Liverpool & North West

With over 25 years of experience, I've built a reputation for capturing the extraordinary in every moment. From the high-energy world of professional wrestling to intimate portrait sessions, my work spans the full spectrum of human experience.

Based in Liverpool, I serve the entire North West region, bringing professional-grade equipment and artistic vision to every shoot. My wrestling photography has gained recognition throughout the UK, while my portrait work includes prestigious literary figures such as Ramsey Campbell - Britain's most respected living horror writer and author of over 30 novels.

My client portfolio includes internationally renowned authors, professional wrestlers, community leaders, and creative professionals who trust me to capture their authentic essence and professional image.
