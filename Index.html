About Tony Knox

Wrestling Photography Specialist - North West

With over 25 years of experience, I've built a reputation as the North West's premier wrestling photographer. From WWE events to independent promotions, my work captures the raw emotion, athleticism, and theatrical artistry that makes professional wrestling compelling.

Based in the North West, I bring professional-grade equipment and an intimate understanding of wrestling dynamics to every shoot. My wrestling photography portfolio features Rey Mysterio and other wrestling legends, while my diverse portfolio includes prestigious literary figures such as Ramsey Campbell - Britain's most respected living horror writer.

My client portfolio spans from professional wrestling organizations to internationally renowned authors, community leaders, and creative professionals who trust me to capture their authentic essence and professional image with technical excellence.
